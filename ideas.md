# Azelia Studio — Sales Page Design Direction

## Chosen Direction: Quiet Luxury Editorial

Warm parchment ground, sage structural accent, olive dark, terracotta urgency.
Cormorant Garamond (display) + DM Sans (body).

### Design Movement
Quiet Luxury Editorial — the aesthetic language of premium design studios, architecture magazines, and high-end service businesses.

### Core Principles
1. Restraint as confidence. Whitespace is not emptiness — it signals that the brand does not need to shout.
2. Typography does the heavy lifting. Contrast between refined serif display and clean sans-serif body.
3. Warmth over sterility. Beige and warm off-white backgrounds, olive and sage as structural accents.
4. Urgency through specificity. Scarcity signals are typeset quietly but precisely.

### Color Philosophy
- Ground: `#F5F0E8` — warm parchment
- Ink: `#2C2416` — deep warm brown
- Sage: `#7A8C6E` — structural accent, signature brand colour
- Olive: `#4A5240` — dark structural, nav, dark sections
- Terracotta: `#B5613A` — urgency accent ONLY
- Cream white: `#FDFAF4` — card backgrounds
- Gold: `#C9A96E` — premium emphasis, "Most popular" badge

### Layout Paradigm
Asymmetric editorial columns. Hero left-weighted. Sections alternate alignment. Pricing: Signature card slightly larger.

### Signature Elements
1. Thin horizontal rules (1px sage) as section separators.
2. Oversized italic Cormorant Garamond numerals in light sage — recurring Azelia brand motif in Problem and Process sections.
3. Inline terracotta urgency pills — small, precise, never dominant.

### Typography System
- Display: Cormorant Garamond 600, slightly loose tracking
- Body: DM Sans 400/500
- H1: `clamp(3rem, 7.5vw, 6rem)` / H2: `clamp(2.2rem, 5vw, 4rem)` / Body: `1rem/1.75`

### Brand Essence
Azelia Studio — bespoke website design for design-led businesses that want enquiries, not just compliments.
Personality: Calm. Precise. Trusted.

### Brand Voice
Confident expert speaking directly to one person. Never a pitch, always a conversation.
Example: "Your work gets noticed. Let's make it get booked."
Banned: "Welcome to Azelia Studio", "Get started today", "Revolutionary".

---

## Style Decisions

- No black backgrounds — use `#2C2416` (warm ink) or `#4A5240` (olive) for dark sections.
- Ticker bar uses terracotta `#B5613A` background, positioned AFTER the hero (not as the first element).
- The first visible brand moment is calm and editorial: AS botanical monogram + Azelia Studio name + hero headline lead before any urgency message.
- Urgency may use terracotta, but only as a small inline signal or restrained label — never as the dominant first-impression element.
- Pricing section: Signature card slightly larger (1.08fr), gold badge, sage border. Savings shown as editorial text, not a coupon badge. Terracotta only for the scarcity dot + text.
- Guarantee block: positioned below the primary CTA button, inside the Signature pricing card background.
- Imagery comes from one tactile editorial world: muted interiors, wedding tablescapes, material boards, sketches, natural studio light. No generic laptop/office stock.
- Oversized italic Cormorant numerals (opacity ~0.18-0.22) appear in both Problem and Process sections as the recurring Azelia brand motif.
- The AS botanical monogram appears prominently in the navbar (44px), as a decorative ghost mark in the hero (desktop only), and in the footer.
